The AI Scoring Engine

Our hybrid engine evaluates applicants across three distinct layers to ensure accuracy, fairness, and depth.

finalScore = (Deterministic × 0.45) + (AI Reasoning × 0.40) + (RAG Context × 0.15)

45% Deterministic

Skill overlap, experience proximity, project technical density, and clarity signals.

40% AI Reasoning

Holistic evaluation via GPT-4o-mini against the specific role culture and requirements.

15% RAG Context

Vector similarity analysis between resume chunks and role descriptions using FAISS.
